Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Deliteo collects, uses and protects the personal information you may provide through our application.

1. Information we collect

Deliteo does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address or location. The app only stores data related to your recipe preferences, ingredients and settings locally on your device.

2. How we use the information

The data collected is used exclusively to provide features such as:

  • Saving your recipes and shopping lists.
  • Remembering your dietary preferences and allergens.
  • Personalizing the in-app experience.

3. Local storage

All information is stored locally on your device. It is not sent to external servers or shared with third parties.

4. Permissions

The app may request optional access to certain system functionalities, such as local storage, solely to enhance app functionality.

5. Changes to this policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating this section.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, you can write to us at contact@deliteo.com.

By using Deliteo, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
